About Us

Established in 2006 and South Australian owned and operated, Accelerate Group are specialist finance brokers catering for all finance needs.

  1. Home
  2. About Us
At Accelerate Loans, we strive for excellence in all that we do and look forward to helping to secure your financial future. We believe that loan products should be easy to understand, easy to access and allow you peace of mind.

You should think of us as a professional resource in the same way you think of your accountant, financial planner or solicitor, as we will always tailor solutions in the best interest of you, or your business.

We will work with you and your service providers (real estate agent, conveyancer, and accountant), while ensuring we help you obtain the finance that best suits your circumstances and needs.

When you call Accelerate Loans, you will speak to a real person and we will guide you through your loan process, providing you with information and support every step of the way.


Why Choose Us


Whether you are purchasing a home for the first time, you are a seasoned investor, or a small business operator, we are committed to providing you with the right loan to meet your needs.

We will also provide you with ongoing customer service that will keep you informed, and assist you in your financial fitness to make informed decisions to reach your goals.

With a panel of over 20 lenders to choose from, we have the ability to find a lender that suits your situation.

Our professional and personalised customer service is what we want you to remember and tell your friends, so our commitment to you is:

  • We will be easy to do business with
  • You will talk to a real person
  • We will get back to you
  • We will listen to what you want
  • We will provide a loan that suits your needs
  • We will provide attractive interest rates
  • We will go the extra mile, every time
A broker will shop around to get each customer the best deal for their financial situation.
